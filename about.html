Our Story

How P3 Workspaces grew from an idea into Gujarat's trusted workspace partner.

P3 Workspaces was founded with a clear purpose: to give entrepreneurs, freelancers, and growing businesses access to a credible, professionally managed address — without the overhead of a traditional office lease. We saw that too many promising startups were struggling with GST registration, bank verifications, and MCA compliance simply because they lacked a recognized business address.

We solved that. Starting from the iconic Nazarbaug Palace in the heart of Vadodara, we built a workspace that blends heritage prestige with modern business infrastructure. Every plan comes with complete documentation support — lease agreements, NOCs, notary stamps — delivered within 48 hours.

Today, with a second location in Naranpura, Ahmedabad, P3 Workspaces serves 100+ clients across Gujarat, from solo consultants to registered companies. Our mission remains the same: make professional workspace accessible, compliant, and affordable for every business at every stage.
